Our Story

In 2001 Gary Larsen passed away from Leukemia, a man of infectuous spirit, best friend, husband and a father who inspired everyone and anyone who crossed his path. We strive to create a sense of giving, caring and love, just as Gary did throughout his life. Our foundation yearn to celebrate the life of a wonderful man by giving to research institutions and other charitable efforts, and to this day, have raised over $100,000 for children with cancer with the aid of our annual fundraiser.

How we help

The Gary Larsen Foundation was founded on passion, love, and the strength of friendship. Our profits go to aid children and their families in the fight against childhood cancer. Join us in our efforts to improve the lives of children everywhere. All of our proceeds go to Make a Wish Foundation, Ronald McDonald House, Happiness is Camping, and the Leukemia Lymphoma Society.
